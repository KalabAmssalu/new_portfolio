## Problem

A browser-only ERP cannot capture wet signatures, physical drop-off, or staffed service desks. Citizens and clerks interact with **devices and cabinets**, not just forms on a screen.

## Tech stack

- **Web platform** as control plane; **device SDKs / drivers** and local agents where needed
- **Digital e-signature** pads, **biometric kiosks**, **smart document cabinets**
- Secure session and handoff patterns between kiosk UI and central services

## What I built

- Integration paths from **modern web UIs** to **physical hardware**: signing ceremonies, identity capture at kiosks, and cabinet open/close and chain-of-custody events tied to case records.
- Error handling and offline-tolerant flows so front desks stay operational when networks degrade.
- Coordination with vendors and field teams to stabilize deployments across sites.

## Outcome

True **full-stack delivery**: software that matches how work actually happens in government offices — not “digital transformation” that ignores the desk, the line, and the cabinet.
