## Problem

Driving schools track students, instructors, vehicles, schedules, exams, and compliance paperwork. Spreadsheets and disconnected tools create double entry and missed renewals.

## Tech stack

- Custom **web application** with role-based access for admin, instructors, and students
- **Reporting** for progress, test readiness, and regulatory exports

## What I built

- **Project leadership** from initial analysis through delivery: requirements, data model, UX priorities, and release.
- Core modules for **scheduling**, **progress tracking**, and **operational reporting** tailored to local regulations and school workflows.

## Outcome

A **single system of record** for school operations — fewer errors, faster answers for students and parents, and leadership visibility without manual consolidation.
