## Problem

Ethiopia lacked a dedicated business review and testimonial platform. Businesses differ wildly in what they want to display; rigid tables would mean endless migrations for every new attribute.

## Tech stack

- **Microservices** with **gRPC** between core services
- **RabbitMQ** for asynchronous events (notifications, search indexing, moderation)
- **PostgreSQL** with **Entity-Attribute-Value (EAV)** for flexible profiles

## What I built

- End-to-end delivery as lead analyst and engineer: domain model, service boundaries, and client applications.
- **gRPC** interfaces for low-latency internal calls; **RabbitMQ** for decoupled side effects and resilience under load.
- **EAV modeling** so businesses could define custom fields without schema churn — with indexing and query strategies to keep common paths fast.

## Outcome

**Ethiopia’s first** dedicated testimonial platform — technically able to absorb diverse business types through **dynamic data modeling** instead of one-size-fits-all columns.
