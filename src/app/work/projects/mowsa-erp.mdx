## Problem

A national ministry operated on paper-heavy processes: slow turnaround, limited traceability, and heavy staff load for routine case and document handling.

## Tech stack

- **Backend-first** ERP services with strong authorization and audit
- **Integration** with digital signatures, document storage, and kiosk-facing flows (see companion hardware case study)
- **Agile** delivery with a large cross-functional team

## What I built

- **Backend architecture** for a nationwide ERP covering core ministerial workflows end to end.
- Digitization of forms, approvals, and records so staff work from a single system of record.
- Close collaboration with analysts and UX so regulatory requirements translated into enforceable system rules.

## Outcome

Roughly **95% reduction in manual paperwork** for targeted processes — faster service delivery, better reporting for leadership, and a foundation for continued digital government expansion.
