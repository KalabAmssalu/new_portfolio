## Problem

A combined hiring marketplace and education product needed high availability: spikes during campaigns, independent scaling of media-heavy workloads, and safe, frequent releases.

## Tech stack

- **Microservices** with clear bounded contexts
- **Docker** & **Kubernetes** for orchestration
- **AWS** and on-premises capacity where required
- **Jenkins** & **GitHub Actions** for CI/CD

## What I built

- **Decoupled microservices** so core marketplace, media, and learning modules could evolve and scale independently.
- **Cloud and hybrid ops**: production paths on AWS with attention to cost, observability, and failure domains.
- **Automated pipelines** to shorten release cycles and reduce human error during deploys.
- **Learning Academy** module embedded in the same platform for video production training.

## Outcome

A platform posture built for **uptime and iteration**: containerized services, orchestrated rollouts, and pipelines that made continuous delivery the default rather than a special event.
