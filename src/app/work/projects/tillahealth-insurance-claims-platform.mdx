## Problem

Medical claims and eligibility touch many parties — insurers, providers, clearinghouses — with strict formats and high volume. Manual handling does not scale; errors and delays directly affect members and cash flow.

## Tech stack

- **Exchange:** EDI standards for secure medical data interchange
- **Domains:** Member & provider portals, encounter capture, clearinghouse automation, core policy/claims, filtering agents
- **Platform:** Scalable multi-tenant services with auditability and integration hooks

## What I built

- End-to-end **insurance infrastructure**: portals for members and providers, encounter pipelines, and a central **clearing house** to automate claim intake and routing.
- **EDI** pipelines for predictable, standards-based exchange with partners instead of ad hoc file drops.
- **Centralized filtering agents** to enforce business rules, detect anomalies, and reduce rework before claims hit core processing.

## Outcome

A **high-scale, multi-tenant** foundation that moves claims from paper and manual steps toward **automated, traceable processing** — improving turnaround and operational visibility across the ecosystem.
