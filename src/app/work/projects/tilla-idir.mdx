## Problem

Idir groups coordinate mutual support through in-person processes and informal ledgers. Scaling participation, transparency, and payouts across members required a trustworthy digital layer without losing cultural trust.

## Tech stack

- **Mobile-first** product with secure payments and member identity
- **Backend** services for contributions, events, and financial reconciliation
- **Notifications** and lightweight admin tools for group leaders

## What I built

- Project-managed delivery of a **first-of-its-kind** digital Idir experience for the Ethiopian community.
- Flows for **community gatherings**, **contributions**, and transparent tracking of pooled resources.
- Emphasis on simplicity for members who are not power users, with guardrails for treasurers and admins.

## Outcome

**Digitized traditional community social security** — reducing friction for contributions and improving visibility for members while preserving the social fabric Idir is built on.
