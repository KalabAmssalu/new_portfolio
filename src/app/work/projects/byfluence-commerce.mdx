## Problem

Merchants needed Shopify-class commerce plus dropshipping and **print-on-demand** in one place — without treating security as an afterthought when handling sensitive customer and payment data.

## Tech stack

- **React** & **Node.js** for storefront and service tier
- **Custom POD** pipeline: design upload, fulfillment routing, vendor integration
- **Security:** HIPAA-level posture where applicable, encryption for transactions and PII

## What I built

- Directed product and engineering for **Byfluence** — integrated **dropshipping**, **retail**, and **POD**.
- Automation for **product customization** and handoff to production partners.
- Strong **data protection**: encryption in transit and at rest, least-privilege access, and review of third-party touchpoints.

## Outcome

A differentiated **local-first commerce platform** combining marketplace mechanics with POD — plus a security story that holds up when regulators and partners ask hard questions.
