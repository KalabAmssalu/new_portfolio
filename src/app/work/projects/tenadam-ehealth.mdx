## Problem

Patients and clinicians needed a single entry point for remote care: live visits, longitudinal homecare support, and legally sound prescribing — without compromising privacy or availability.

## Tech stack

- **Realtime:** WebRTC-style **RTC** for video and audio consultations
- **Product:** Super-app modules for homecare, scheduling, and prescriptions
- **Security:** Hardened flows for e-prescription issuance and audit trails

## What I built

- Lead architecture and project management for a **telehealth super application**.
- Integrated **real-time video/audio** consultations with session quality and access control appropriate for clinical use.
- **Homecare** workflows for tracking and continuity outside the clinic.
- **E-prescription** engine designed for regulatory and safety constraints around who can prescribe, sign, and fulfill.

## Outcome

A **unified digital front door** for care delivery that connects real-time visits, ongoing homecare, and prescribing in one coherent product instead of three disconnected tools.
